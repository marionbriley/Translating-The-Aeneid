The Aeneid in Translation

Sources

Latin

Latin excerpts came from a TheLatinLibrary copy of the original Aeneid text 'Aeneidos' by Publius Vergilius Maro.

English

Our English excerpts came from Perseus, translated by Theodore C. Williams in 1910.

German

Our German excerpts came from 'Die Gedichte des P. Virgilius Maro, III: Aeneis', translated by W. Hertberg in 1859.

Spanish

Our Spanish excerpts came from a University of California digital scan of "La Eneida," translated by D. Luis Herrera y Robles in 1905.
